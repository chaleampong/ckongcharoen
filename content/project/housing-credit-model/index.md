As principal investigator, developed a forecasting model for housing mortgage credit
for the Government Housing Bank (ธนาคารอาคารสงเคราะห์) and the broader Thai banking
system. The model supports strategic planning and risk management by projecting loan
demand under various macroeconomic scenarios, incorporating interest rate dynamics,
income growth, and housing market conditions.
