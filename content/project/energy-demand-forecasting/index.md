Submitted to Thailand's Energy Policy and Planning Office (สำนักงานนโยบายและแผนพลังงาน),
these two consecutive projects built forecasting models to support long-term energy
conservation planning:

- **Transport Sector Energy Demand Forecasting (2013):** Developed econometric models
  to forecast fuel and energy consumption across transport modes, with BERA Co. Ltd.,
  to support energy conservation targets in the transport sector.

- **Long-Term Electricity Demand Forecasting (2014):** Surveyed and updated electricity
  demand forecasting models for long-term conservation planning across economic sectors
  (with Suppawat Rungsuriyawibool and team).
