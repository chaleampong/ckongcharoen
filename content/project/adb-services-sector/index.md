Commissioned by the Asian Development Bank (ADB), this project examined the critical
constraints — regulatory, institutional, and structural — that impede the growth and
competitiveness of Thailand's services sector. The analysis provided evidence-based
recommendations to support services sector reform and economic diversification policy.
