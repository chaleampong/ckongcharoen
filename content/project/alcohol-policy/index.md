As principal investigator, led two major policy evaluation projects on alcohol regulation
in Thailand, funded by the Thai Health Promotion Foundation (สสส.) and the Centre for
Alcohol Studies (ศวส.):

- **CPTPP and Alcohol Consumption (2021):** Assessed the projected impact of Thailand
  joining the Comprehensive and Progressive Agreement for Trans-Pacific Partnership
  (CPTPP) on alcohol consumption, modelling how trade liberalisation would affect
  domestic alcohol markets and public health outcomes.

- **Alcohol Deregulation Impact Assessment (2023):** Evaluated the economic, health,
  and social consequences of three proposed deregulation scenarios: (1) allowing
  small-scale alcohol producers, (2) relaxing time-of-sale restrictions, and
  (3) designating special alcohol tourism zones. A related journal article was
  published in the *International Journal of Health Economics and Management* (2025).
  DOI: 10.1007/s10754-025-09395-6
