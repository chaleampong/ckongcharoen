This project studied the regulatory framework and practical guidelines for governing
electricity service tariff rates under Thailand's Energy Industry Act B.E. 2550
(พระราชบัญญัติการประกอบกิจการพลังงาน พ.ศ. 2550). The work reviewed international
regulatory practices, analysed cost-of-service and performance-based rate-setting
approaches, and developed recommendations for strengthening tariff oversight
(with Nilubon Lerthnuwat and team).
