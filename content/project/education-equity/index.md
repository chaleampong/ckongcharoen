In collaboration with Assoc. Prof. Dr. Thorn Pitidol and team, conducted two related
research projects on educational equity in Thailand for the Equity Fund for Education
(กองทุนเพื่อความเสมอภาคทางการศึกษา / EEF):

- **Equity-Based Budgeting for Education (2021):** Studied approaches for designing
  integrated education budgeting systems that prioritise equity outcomes
  (Equity-based Budgeting for Education), providing a framework for targeting
  public education spending toward disadvantaged groups
  (with Chaiyut Panyasawatsutti and team).

- **Policy Dynamics of Educational Equity in Thailand (2025, ongoing):** Examines
  the evolving policy landscape for educational equity in Thailand over the decade
  2015–2025, tracking policy commitments, implementation gaps, and the political
  economy of reform — supporting evidence-based advocacy for inclusive education policy.
