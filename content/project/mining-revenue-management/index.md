Submitted to the Department of Primary Industries and Mines (กรมอุตสาหกรรมพื้นฐานและการเหมืองแร่),
this project designed a systematic framework for managing and improving the collection of
government revenue from mineral extraction activities under the Minerals Act B.E. 2510.
The work focused on setting criteria and rules for mineral royalty collection
(with Kriangkrai Techakannont and Suppawat Rungsuriyawibool).
