Provided analytical and systems design services to Thailand's Budget Bureau
(สำนักงบประมาณ) across two projects:

- **Econometric Model for Budget Strategy (2018):** Developed a macroeconomic
  econometric model for the Bureau's Budget Strategy Division (กองยุทธศาสตร์การงบประมาณ)
  to support evidence-based fiscal planning and budget allocation decisions
  (with Satra Sudsawat and team).

- **New e-Budgeting System Design (2022):** Analysed and designed the conceptual
  framework for a new electronic budget management system (New e-Budgeting), covering
  system architecture, workflow design, and data integration requirements to modernise
  Thailand's public financial management infrastructure.
