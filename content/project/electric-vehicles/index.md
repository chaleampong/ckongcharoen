Submitted to Thailand's Energy Conservation Fund (กองทุนอนุรักษ์พลังงาน), this project
examined economic and policy instruments to promote electric vehicle (EV) adoption.
The study covered demand-side fiscal incentives, charging infrastructure planning,
regulatory barriers, and international comparisons of EV promotion strategies to develop
actionable policy recommendations for Thailand's low-carbon transport transition.
