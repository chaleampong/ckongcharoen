Submitted to the Public Debt Management Office (สำนักบริหารหนี้สาธารณะ), these two
projects assessed the economic returns and spillover effects of major public infrastructure
investments in Thailand:

- **Transport Infrastructure Strategy Macroeconomic Model (2016):** Built a macroeconomic
  model to assess the economy-wide impact of Thailand's transport infrastructure
  development strategy, as part of a broader project management consultancy
  (with Thatri Jantarakolika and team).

- **Rail Mass Transit Economic Impact Assessment (2018):** Estimated the economic
  effects of rail transport investment for use in planning future transit projects,
  focusing on multiplier effects and long-run productivity gains from improved connectivity.
