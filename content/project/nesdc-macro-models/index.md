Provided macroeconomic modelling services to the National Economic and Social Development
Council (สำนักงานคณะกรรมการพัฒนาการเศรษฐกิจและสังคมแห่งชาติ / สภาพัฒน์) across two projects:

- **ASEAN Integration and Inequality (2015):** Used economic modelling to assess how
  Thailand's participation in the ASEAN Economic Community affects income inequality,
  and developed recommendations for strengthening social protection systems
  (with Arayah Preechametta and team).

- **Current Quarter Model (CQM) for GDP Nowcasting (2016):** Developed and refined
  a Current Quarter Model for estimating quarterly economic growth in near real-time,
  supporting NESDC's macroeconomic monitoring and planning functions
  (with Pranee Tinakorn and team).
