Submitted to the Office of the National Higher Education Science Research and
Innovation Policy Council (สกสว. / NRIIS), this project developed a social monitoring
system to track the social dimensions and distributional impacts of research and
innovation funding in Thailand. The framework draws on social indicator methodology
to support evidence-based evaluation of public investment in science and innovation
(with Assoc. Prof. Dr. Thorn Pitidol and team).
