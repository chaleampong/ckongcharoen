I teach courses at both undergraduate and graduate levels at the 
Faculty of Economics, Thammasat University, covering econometrics, 
financial time series, forecasting, and research methodology.

* ##### [EE628 Time-Series Econometrics]({{< ref "/courses/ec628" >}})
  Graduate course covering time-series econometrics models and applications.

* ##### [EC435 Introduction to Financial Econometrics]({{< ref "/courses/ec435" >}})
  Undergraduate course on financial time series and econometric modelling.

* ##### [MB634 Business, Finance, and Economic Analysis and Forecast]({{< ref "/courses/mb634" >}})
  MBE course on statistical forecasting methods for business and economic variables.

* ##### [EC215 Research Methodology]({{< ref "/courses/ec215" >}})
  Undergraduate course on economics research writing and methodology.
